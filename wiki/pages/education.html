Starlight Bazaar - Creative activities for all ages

"On the evening of May 18, NJU-China participated in the Nanjing University's Starlight Bazaar. NJU-China actively interacts with the participating retired teachers, active teachers, their family members and students to share their knowledge of synthetic biology and popularize the importance of synthetic biology.We carried out a charity sale and sold dozens of handbags, badges and creative hand-painted stickers, with a total of more than 400 yuan. In the volunteer teaching activity we participated in in July, we used all the proceeds of the charity sale to buy cultural creations and gifts for children in Suqian."

星光集市

"In total, hundreds of participants participated in our mini-game, "Design the creature you most want to create." Through this activity and our speech, we let more abiotic background teachers and students understand what synthetic biology is, and also planted biological kindling for many children who may participate in the field of life science in the future. This is in line with our team spirit "Prometheus" - the transmission of the fire of life to humanity."

Volunteer teaching in Suqian -- Open up the vision of rural children

"From July 6th to July 9th, we went to Yuelai Town, Suqian City to carry out a four-day volunteer teaching activity. We discussed artificial intelligence, medical applications of synthetic biology with the kids, and introduced them to synthetic biology through the new-hand version of our Prometheus model.Using clay as an innovative teaching aid, we led the children to learn the structure of yeast and let them create their dream creatures.The following is our volunteer teaching diary."

July 6 Weather: Cloudy to light rain Recorded by: Shiyuan Chen

"At 7:30 am on July 6, we set out for Yuelai Town, Shuyang County, Suqian City, Jiangsu Province. After a more than four hours' journey, we arrive at Yuelai nine-year School in Shuyang County. The children warmly welcome us."

支教1

"Today is the first day of volunteer teaching. We introduce synthetic biology and artificial intelligence to two classes in Grade 7, and hold the "Synthetic Biology and Artificial Intelligence Knowledge Competition" activity. In addition, we also discusse the application scenarios and advantages and disadvantages of artificial intelligence with the students, and the students also propose the application of artificial intelligence in biology and medicine, such as the application of artificial intelligence robots in tumor treatment."

July 7 Weather: Heavy rain Recorded by: Zecheng Guan

July 8 Weather: Fog Recorded by: Yourong Shi

July 9 Weather: Cloudy Recorded by: Feng He

"This year, we plan to use iGEM's component database to train an AI model that will use existing components to transform an input plasmid function into multiple plasmids that have the potential to fulfill that function, while giving the base sequences of the individual components in the plasmid, the database number, and so on. We will go through the following process to build and refine our AI model. (workflow) First, we will organize the database accumulated by iGEM for 20 years into the form of a knowledge graph, and use manual tagging to train the AI model to classify and summarize the data. Next, we will use this knowledge graph to build （train?）an AI model, the function of which is to input the desired function of the plasmid, and the model will give multiple plasmids that can potentially fulfill the function, and at the same time give the sequence of each element base in the plasmid, the database number, etc. Our model will also provide a newcomer mode, so that the general public who has not been exposed to synthetic biology can input vague requirements, the model will give multiple plasmids that can potentially fulfill the function, and will also give how to use the plasmid, as well as detailed information about the function and components of the plasmid, which will make it more convenient for the general public to get in touch with synthetic biology and use it. According to the needs of the training dataset and the knowledge graph dataset required by our model, we are going to give a software to fill in the iGEM database in a standardized way, which only needs to fill in the data according to the instructions to make the data filled in the database become a high-quality corpus. In the long run, the iGEM database can become a high-quality synthetic biology corpus database, and the data in it can be directly used to train AI models. This will make it easier for us to optimize and iterate our models, and for other iGEMers or others to train AI models. In turn, this will facilitate the whole synthetic biology to embrace AI."
