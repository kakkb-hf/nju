Initial Design - Knowledge Graph

Goal: Same as project goal Data Source: Web scraping data (parts.igem.org), no cleaning Graph Design:

  • Node Types: (Category (catalog), Component (part))
  • Internal Attributes: (Webpage original text (webpage), using html2txt)
  • Relationships: ([Component] belongs to [Category], [Component] and [Component] are twins or uses)

Issues:

  • Internal attributes are chaotic and cannot accurately describe the functions and characteristics of components (1.0)
  • The network structure of relationships is too simple, making global indexing and related reasoning inconvenient (1.0)

V1.0

Improvements:

  • Changed internal attributes to (webpage cleaned and extracted description (ChatGLM3), nucleotide sequence (sequence)) (0.0)
  • Used Sent2Vec for global matching (0.0)

Highlights:

  • Introduced LLM model: Sent2Vec can achieve semantic fuzzy matching, and GLM3 can remove irrelevant content from the webpage.

Issues:

  • Extraction results have inconsistent formats and lengths, making it difficult to capture key content (1.1)
  • Cannot handle long texts (2.3)
